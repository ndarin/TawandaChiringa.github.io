Optimisation modeling using stock data

The aim was to develop an optimisation model that answers the question of product, brand and market selection. Companies like Unilever that have hundreds of brands and thousands of products, rely on optimisation to raise the overall value of their portfolios to get the highest returns. Portfolio optimisation can be used in conjunction with price (cost) analysis to assess the overall impact on value of price (or cost) adjustments to a product portfolio. Business strategy insights that can be gained include niche market selection, risk exposure analysis (over exposure to a product), market entry or exits and so on. Although the model was developed using stock data, it can be applied to any company. *This is based on an actual work deliverable - names have been altered. See "More details"
